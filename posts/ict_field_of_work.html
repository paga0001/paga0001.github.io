What did I do?

In a nutshell, my work was divided to database manager (DM) and head of technical support.
My responsibilities were for example,
team managing, important data requests, training interns, managing database operations,
sending information for the Finnish IRS from our end-customer earnings.
We started sending the data using GitBash and it took 8 hours per week to send all the salary + expense data using that method.
That doesn't include the amount of time that took to get the response from IRS and importing the data to the database.

What did my usual tasks were as a database manager?

Well usually the database manager is a person who is behind the scenes
and makes sure that the data that comes from a website, for example, is stored correctly to the database.
My work as something similar, but it was mostly for finding issues on the production
and fixing them while telling the developers about the bugs that they have created so that our work wouldn't be too boring.
I was also responsible to the board and CEO to get their data requests, as our BIG Data wasn't yet fully functional.
